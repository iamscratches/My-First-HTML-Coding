THE FIRST GENERATION COMPUTERS ARE KNOWN TO BE THE OLDEST AND MOST EXPENSIVE COMPUTERS IN THE GENERATIONS,HOWEVER THE QUALITY AND EXISTENCE IS VERY LESS

THE FIRST GENERATION COMPUTERS[1940-1956]
CHARACTERISTICS
  • THE FIRST GENERATION COMPUTERS USED VACCUM TUBES FOR CIRCUITRY.

  • MAGNETIC DRUMS WERE USED FOR MEMORY.

  • THEY WERE ENORMOUS MACHINES TAKING UP ENTIRE ROOMS.

  • THEY WERE VERY EXVENSIVE,USED A GREAT DEAL OF ELECTRICITY AND GENERATED A LOT OF HEAT.

  • INPUTS WAS BASED ON PUNCHED CARDS.

  • OUTPUTS WAS IN THE FORM OF PRINTOUTS.

    EXAMPLES OF FIRST GENERATION COMPUTERS:-

    • UNIVERSAL AUTOMATIC COMPUTER[UNIVAC]
    • ELECTRONIC NUMERICAL INTEGRATOR AND CALCULATOR[ENIAC]
    • ELECTRONIC DELAY STORAGE AUTOMATIC[EDSAC]
