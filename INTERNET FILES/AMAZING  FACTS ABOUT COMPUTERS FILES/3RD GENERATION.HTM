THE THIRD GENERATION COMPUTERS[1964-1971]
CHARACTERISTICS
  • THE INTEGRATED CIRCUIT[I.C] WAS THE HALLMARK OF THE THIRD GENERATION OF COMPUTERS.

  • TRANSISTORS WERE MADE IN SMALL SIZE AND PUT INTO SILICON CHIPS HAVING THE SIZE OF THE POSTAGE STAMPS.THIS INCREASED THE SPEED AND EFFICIENCY OF THE COMPUTERS.

  • USERS INTERACTED WITH THE THIRD GENERATION COMPUTERS THROUGH KEYBOARDS AND MONITORS.

  • OPERATING SYSTEMS WERE DEVELOPED.
