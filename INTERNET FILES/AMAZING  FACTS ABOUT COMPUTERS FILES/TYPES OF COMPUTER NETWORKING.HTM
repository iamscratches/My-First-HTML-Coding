CLASSIFICATION OF COMPUTER NETWORKING

TYPES OF COMPUTER NETWORKING COMPUTER NETWORKS CAN BE CLASSIFIED INTO DIFFERENT CATAGORIES
BASED ON THE FOLLOWING :
  • THE AREA THEY WERE COVERING
  • THE ARCHITECTUREOF THE NETWORK
  • THE TOPOLOGY
  • THE MEDIUM OF TRANSFER
