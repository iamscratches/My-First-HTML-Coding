LOCAL AREA NETWORK(LAN)

LAN A LAN IS A NETWORK COVERING A SMALL GEOGRAPHIC AREA ,LIKE A HOME,SCHOOL,OFFICE OR BUILDING.CURRENT LAN'S ARE MOSTLY TO BE BASED ON ETHERNET TECHNOLOGY.THE NETWORK IN YOUR BANK OR SCHOOL LAB IS AN EXAMPLE OF LAN.COAXIAL CABLES ARE USUALLY USED TO CONNECT THE COMPUTERS IN A LAN.
